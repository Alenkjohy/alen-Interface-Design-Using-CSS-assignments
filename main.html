Sweet Facts

Snickers was first introduced in 1930 by Mars Inc. It is one of the best-selling candy bars worldwide.

Named after the Mars family's favorite horse, it quickly became a fan favorite.

Snickers' iconic slogan "You're Not You When You're Hungry" has been featured in numerous commercials.
