Precision Engineering & Design

The MIDO Baroncelli Chronometer represents the pinnacle of Swiss watchmaking, blending timeless aesthetics with certified precision. This exceptional timepiece offers:

Certified Precision

Officially certified as a chronometer by the COSC (Contrôle Officiel Suisse des Chronomètres), guaranteeing exceptional accuracy within -4/+6 seconds per day. The automatic movement features 25 jewels and a 48-hour power reserve.

Elegant Design

The silver sunray dial features rose gold-tone applied indices and elegant leaf-shaped hands. The multi-level dial architecture includes:

  • Day display at 12 o'clock
  • Date window at 3 o'clock
  • Small seconds subdial at 6 o'clock
  • Chronograph 30-minute counter at 9 o'clock

Premium Materials

The 42mm case is crafted from 316L stainless steel with rose gold PVD coating, paired with a black genuine leather strap featuring alligator pattern embossing. The sapphire crystal with anti-reflective coating ensures optimal readability.

Technical Specifications

  • Caliber: MIDO 1320 (based on ETA C07.621)
  • Frequency: 21,600 vibrations/hour (3Hz)
  • Water resistance: 50m (5 bar)
  • Crystal: Scratch-resistant sapphire with AR coating
  • Case thickness: 11.2mm
  • Lug width: 21mm
