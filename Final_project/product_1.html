Masterful Skeleton Design

The Hamilton Viewmatic Skeleton showcases the intricate beauty of mechanical watchmaking through its expertly skeletonized movement. This exceptional timepiece reveals:

Movement & Engineering

At its heart beats the Hamilton H-10-S automatic movement, featuring:

  • 25 jewels for reduced friction
  • 80-hour power reserve
  • 3Hz (21,600 vibrations/hour) frequency
  • Nivachron™ balance spring for anti-magnetic properties
The skeletonized bridges and mainplate are decorated with perlage and Geneva stripes, showcasing Swiss finishing techniques.

Design Details

The 40mm rose gold-tone case frames a mesmerizing dial that reveals:

  • Partially skeletonized hour and minute hands
  • Rose gold-tone applied indices
  • Small seconds subdial at 6 o'clock
  • Exhibition case back showing the decorated movement

Materials & Construction

Premium materials ensure both beauty and durability:

  • 316L stainless steel case with rose gold PVD coating
  • Scratch-resistant sapphire crystal on both front and back
  • Genuine black leather strap with contrast stitching
  • Signed stainless steel deployment clasp

Technical Specifications

  • Lug width: 20mm
  • Weight: 78 grams
  • Water resistance: 5 ATM (50 meters)
  • Functions: Hours, minutes, small seconds
