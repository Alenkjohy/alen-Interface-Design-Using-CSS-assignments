Detailed Description

The Ingersoll 1892 Automatic pays homage to the golden age of watchmaking with its exquisite craftsmanship and attention to detail. This limited edition timepiece features:

Movement & Precision

Powered by a reliable automatic movement with 21 jewels, this watch offers precise timekeeping with a power reserve of 42 hours. The exhibition case back reveals the beautifully decorated movement.

Design Elements

The rich brown sunray dial features rose gold-tone hands and hour markers, complemented by three subdials displaying day, date, and 24-hour time. The intricate guilloché pattern at the center adds depth and sophistication.

Materials & Comfort

Crafted from stainless steel with rose gold plating, the 44mm case provides durability while maintaining elegance. The genuine brown leather strap features contrast stitching and a signed buckle for a perfect fit.

Special Features

  • Scratch-resistant mineral crystal
  • 50m water resistance
  • Luminous hands for low-light visibility
  • Limited edition serial number
